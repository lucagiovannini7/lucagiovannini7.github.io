Luca Giovannini

Institut Germanistik, Universität Potsdam · giovannini@uni-potsdam.de

I am a PhD student at the German Studies Department of the University of Potsdam, currently working as a research assistant (wissenschaftliche Hilfskraft) for the DraCor project (CLS-INFRA). I am interested in digital humanities and computational criticism, with a focus on literary networks analysis.

You can read my full CV here. To know more about my doctoral research project, check out my recent publications or take a look at these slides (in German).
