Welcome!

I am Luca Giovannini and I work as research coordinator for the Digital Humanities Network of the University of Potsdam, Germany.

I studied comparative literature in Turin, Warwick, and Cologne, and recently got a joint PhD in the same field (with a strong focus on digital humanities) from the universities of Potsdam and Padova.

I am interested in computational criticism and cultural analytics, and still heavily involved in the DraCor project (CLS-INFRA). My PhD project dealt with the evolution of European drama in the early modern period (1550-1700) and its exploration through computational methods.

You can reach me by email or on Bluesky. You might also check out my institutional webpage and my often outdated official résumés (English/German). Code for all my projects is usually stored in my GitHub repository. My ORCID ID is 0000-0003-2444-7192.
