Welcome!

I am Luca Giovannini and I work as research coordinator (postdoc) for the Digital Humanities Network of the University of Potsdam, Germany.

I studied comparative literature in Turin, Warwick, and Cologne, and recently got a joint PhD in the same field (with a strong focus on digital humanities) from the universities of Potsdam and Padova.

I am interested in computational criticism and cultural analytics, and still heavily involved in the DraCor project. My PhD project dealt with the evolution of European drama in the early modern period (1550-1700) and its exploration through computational methods.

You can reach me by email or on Bluesky. You might also check out my institutional webpage and my often outdated official résumés (English/German).
