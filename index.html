Welcome!

I am Luca Giovannini, a joint PhD student at the German Studies Department of the University of Potsdam and the Department of Linguistic and Literary Studies of the University of Padova. I also work as a research assistant (wissenschaftliche Hilfskraft) for the DraCor project (CLS-INFRA).

I am interested in digital humanities, computational criticism, and cultural analytics, with a focus on literary networks analysis and some experience in text encoding (XML-TEI). My research project deals with the evolution of European drama in the early modern period (1550-1700) and its exploration through computational methods.

You can reach me by email or on Twitter. You might also check out my institutional webpage, my full CV, or my GitHub repository.
